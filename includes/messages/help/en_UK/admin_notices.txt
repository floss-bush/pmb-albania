<h1>Managing records</h1>

<p>The section <b>Managing records</b> allows you to define your records parameters.
<ul>
<li><b>sources</b> allows you to indicate the sources of your records in order to distinguish distribution rights</li>
<li><b>status</b> allows you to indicate any special status attached to records as well as in the OPAC display</li>
<li><b>custom fields</b> enables  you to customize fields for the records</li>
</ul></p>
