<h1>Selective Dissemination of Information module</h1>
<p>SDI is a current awareness system which regularly alerts readers to the latest publications in their field(s) of interest corresponding to their reader's profiles.</p>

<h2>Routing</h2>
Informing readers by email.<br />
<h2>SDI basket</h2>
An SDI basket is where references to newly received or published material are placed. Its content will be routed to readers.<br />
<h2>Equations </h2>
Equations fill in the SDI baskets.<br />
<h2>Classifications </h2>
The classifications will help you organize your baskets and equations.<br />
